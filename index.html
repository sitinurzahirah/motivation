FACTORS TO KEEP MOTIVATE DURING ONLINE EDUCATION

Universites applied online education to student as one of medium to continue the semester that have been hold for a while because of Covid19. Online education which means that the students have to study using online medium such as Google Classroom, Google Meets and Zoom Meeting. The learning session was new to student and also for lecturers but then it slowly can follow up day by day as it becomes a new normal for us during the Covid19.

Uitm also moves to online learning to continue the semester. UiTM Institute of Continuing Education and Professional Studies chief executive officer Professor Dr Azizul Halim Yahya, who is leading the shift to ODL said to reporter Murniati Abu Karim in New Straits Times.

Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) has moved all classes to open and distance learning (ODL) mode effective April 13 until the end of the semester for all its campuses nationwide. All extracurricular activities are also put on hold and all academic activities conducted are a blend of asynchronous (without real-time interaction) and synchronous (real-time interaction) online learning.

Here are some of factors that can keep motivate a student in online education.



1. CREATE A REMINDER



First of all, create a reminder about your task, assignment, deadlines, exam and all important work that required to you. The important thing is your timetable for the whole semester, print out or create because it will be make you on alert when see the timetable. Put all the reminder on the notice board or use whatever that works on and also make sure that it can reach and see clearly. This way will have a clear view of the month ahead. For sure this can make our schedule stay organized and if the plans change we can rearranging it to keep alert and it much easier and less stressful because of the reminder.




2. HAVE A PROPER STUDY SPACE



When you study at home, you need to create or having a study space where you can study and learning properly and give full attention on it. The study space must be in suitable place like in your room or library room in your house as it not disturb you while doing the works or online classes. Organize your study space to give a lift up your motivate by keep the important things only on the study space when learning on the table such as arrange your text book properly, keep the subject's notes in file and also if you need a chair make sure to have a comfortable one as you may spend a lot of time at study space. Study space can make you more focus, ready and consistent in online education and away from distractions.




3. KEEP IN TOUCH WITH YOUR FRIENDS



Study online can be isolating. This sometime make you feel left out in study when you not understand the task or not feel 100 percent of things all of time and you it's better to study with 2 people than alone. This is important to ask around when you are in doubt as if someone is master on that. We still can do discussion or study group even not in meeting face to face but we can video call to have discussion on something that bothering you in study and also help each other out with homework and assignment just don't copy each other. This will be help us in surviving in online education alone.

This quote is so good to share: when I walk along with others, from at least one I will be able to learn.
